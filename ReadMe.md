# 📖 About

**Ease** is an AI-powered mental wellness companion designed to help users navigate everyday emotional challenges through supportive conversations, guided wellness sessions, journaling, and mood tracking.

Unlike traditional AI chatbots, Ease prioritizes **privacy** by running AI directly on the user's device whenever possible, allowing users to access personalized support even without an internet connection.

The goal of Ease is to create a calm, supportive, and distraction-free experience that encourages healthy habits and emotional well-being while respecting user privacy.

> **Disclaimer**
>
> Ease is **not** a replacement for licensed mental health professionals, therapists, psychologists, or emergency services. It is designed to support general wellness and self-reflection.
